THE PROJECT

During the schoolyear of 2019 - 2020, Sarah started a new project. The goal was to pick a few 'total strangers' and try to show them in their intirety through the means of art. This means, through paintings, sketches, soundsrecordings, installations etc. The work show these individuals and the expierences Sarah had with them as an artist and a human being. 

In the process of creating these images of these specific individuals, Sarah came across several dificulties. The expierences with these people were completely opposite from each other. Both of them were not easy, but in a different way. One of the individuals was difficult to work with because they the individual and the artist did not get along easily. This caused a lot of tension in her personal life and her artwork. The other was not easy to portray because they became friends. This made it difficult to separate her personal life from the artwork aswell because when they met up to talk etc. they ended up talking about irrelevant things. As friends do.
