SOFT PASTEL

Besides painting, Sarah has a passion for pastel. When She works with chalk, She quickly becomes more relaxed and creates a feeling within the work. She adds paint and pencil in between to correct herself and bring different materials together. Pastelwork works well together with other materials, in her opinion.
